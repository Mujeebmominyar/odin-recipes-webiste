Mantu

Food Introduction:

Mantu are traditional Afghan dumplings filled with seasoned ground meat and onions, steamed to perfection, and topped with rich sauces. They are one of the most popular dishes in Afghanistan and are often served during gatherings and special occasions.

The dumplings are usually served with a flavorful tomato-based sauce and a creamy yogurt sauce, creating a delicious balance of savory and tangy flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Dumpling wrappers
  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 cloves garlic (for yogurt sauce)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dried mint
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Steps to Cook it:

  1. Prepare the filling by mixing ground meat with chopped onions, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place a small amount of filling into each dumpling wrapper.
  3. Fold and seal the dumplings tightly.
  4. Arrange the dumplings in a steamer.
  5. Steam for 30-40 minutes until fully cooked.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and cook the tomato sauce with garlic and spices.
  7. Mix yogurt with garlic and dried mint to make the yogurt sauce.
  8. Serve the dumplings topped with tomato sauce and yogurt sauce.
